THE 12-WEEK COLLECTION

Week 1: Ground Zero

Hi Rainfall, I decided to write to you weekly till year's end when I might finally leave you and move on with my life (not sure yet).

I will not be sending you links every week, but I'll make sure to publish a new one every week, so check back every Monday to see the next week's letter. I wanted to recap what happened this week but all I can say is “it's well” and “the LORD is in control.”

Last week was such a time for you, how did it go? I'm glad you have recovered from both the injection and malaria. Also, your skin is healing — that's an answered prayer — thank God. Your productivity this week is so beautiful, totally love to see it. It's already making sense, hope you are willing to do more.

Even if you don’t see it now, I push you because I don’t ever want to look back one day and realize the person I started this journey with isn’t there anymore.

That’s why I drag you, remind you, and sometimes even annoy you, because I believe in where we’re heading. I’m not perfect and I’m not even there yet myself, but I know we’ll make it. We’ll pull through.

I pray that you may enjoy good health and that all may go well with you, even as your soul is getting along well. 3 John 1:2
